CHAPTER 5.2. Renewal Notices [500- 500.]
( Chapter 5.2 added by Stats. 1972, Ch. 357. )
500.
Whenever any insurer has, as a regular course of conduct, sent renewal premium notices to an insured, and intends to discontinue that practice, it shall notify such insured of its intention not to send such notices.
(Added by Stats. 1972, Ch. 357.)

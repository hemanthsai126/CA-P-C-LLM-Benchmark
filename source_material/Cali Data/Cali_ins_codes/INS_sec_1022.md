1022.
The notice shall be published in newspapers of general circulation in geographic areas pertinent to the liquidation. The notice shall reference a source, either the liquidated company’s or the liquidator’s Internet Web site, where ongoing information for creditors shall be provided. A copy of the notice, accompanied by an affidavit of due publication, including a statement of the date of publication, shall be filed with the clerk
				of the court.
(Amended by Stats. 2012, Ch. 786, Sec. 13.   (AB 2303)   Effective January 1, 2013.)

ARTICLE 2. Review of Commissioner’s Acts [12940- 12940.]
( Article 2 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
12940.
The acts and orders of the commissioner are subject to such review, or other action by a court of competent jurisdiction, as is permitted or authorized by law.
(Enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145.)

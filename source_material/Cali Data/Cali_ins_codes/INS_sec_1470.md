ARTICLE 10. Policy [1470- 1470.]
( Article 10 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
1470.
In any form of policy prescribed by this code, the attorney may insert provisions or conditions required by the plan of reciprocal or interinsurance if the plan is not inconsistent with, or in conflict with, any law of this State.

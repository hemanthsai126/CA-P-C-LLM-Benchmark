CHAPTER 1. Insurance Adjuster Act [14000 - 14099]

ARTICLE 1. General Provisions [14000 - 14002]
( Article 1 added by Stats. 1980, Ch. 1190, Sec. 11. )
14002.
Nothing in this chapter shall be construed as entitling any person to practice law in this state, unless he is an active member of the State Bar of California.
(Added by Stats. 1980, Ch. 1190, Sec. 11.   Operative July 1, 1981, by Sec. 13 of Ch. 1190.)

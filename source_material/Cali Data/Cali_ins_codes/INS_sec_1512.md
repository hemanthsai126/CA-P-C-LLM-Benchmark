1512.
In addition to the foregoing penalties and where not otherwise provided, the penalty for failure or refusal to comply with any or all of the provisions of this chapter upon the part of the attorney, shall be the refusal, suspension or revocation of certificate of authority. Before such refusal, suspension or revocation, notice and hearing shall be given such attorney in accordance with the procedure provided in Section 704.
(Amended by Stats. 1945, Ch. 901.)

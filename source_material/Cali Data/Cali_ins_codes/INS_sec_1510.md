ARTICLE 13. Penalties [1510 - 1512]
( Article 13 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
1510.
Any attorney-in-fact or representative of such an attorney who exchanges, or solicits or negotiates the exchange of, any contracts of insurance of the kind and character specified in this chapter, except as provided in section 1511, without compliance by the attorney with the provisions of this chapter, is guilty of a misdemeanor.
(Enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145.)

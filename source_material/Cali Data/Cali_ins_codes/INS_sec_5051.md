CHAPTER 5. County Mutual Fire Insurers [5050 - 7060]
( Chapter 5 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
ARTICLE 1. Formation and Organization [5050 - 5063]
( Article 1 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
5051.
Upon the payment in advance of seven thousand fifty-five dollars ($7,055) to the commissioner for all services to be rendered by him or her in the matter of organization of the insurer, those persons shall file with the commissioner a declaration of their intention to incorporate for the purposes expressed in Section 5050. The declaration shall be signed by all of those persons, and shall contain a copy of the articles of incorporation proposed to be adopted.
(Amended by Stats. 2017, Ch. 534, Sec. 54.   (AB 1699)   Effective January 1, 2018.)

CHAPTER 6. Loss [520 - 580]

ARTICLE 5. Certain Insurance Covering Real Property [570 - 572]
( Article 5 added by Stats. 1974, Ch. 288. )
572.
As used in this article, the term “loss payee” shall include, but not be limited to, any mortgagee of the insured real property.
(Added by Stats. 1975, Ch. 281.)

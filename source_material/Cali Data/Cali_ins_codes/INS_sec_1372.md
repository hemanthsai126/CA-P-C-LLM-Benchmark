ARTICLE 5. Finances [1370 - 1375]
( Article 5 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
1372.
Every other exchange shall maintain at all times assets in a sum sufficient to discharge all liabilities and to provide a surplus over all liabilities of fifty thousand dollars ($50,000).
An exchange subject to the provisions of this section is also subject to the provisions of Sections 1370.2, 1370.4, and 1370.8.
(Amended by Stats. 1961, Ch. 454.)

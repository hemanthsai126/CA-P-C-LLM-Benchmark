ARTICLE 4. The Policy [6010 - 6021]
( Article 4 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
6016.
Clauses may be added to the policy providing for and defining the rights, duties and obligations of mortgagees, assignees, and other parties having an interest in, right to or lien upon the insured subject matter.
(Enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145.)

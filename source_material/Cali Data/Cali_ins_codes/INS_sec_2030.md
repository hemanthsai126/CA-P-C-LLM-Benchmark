DIVISION 2. CLASSES OF INSURANCE [1880 - 12880.8]
( Division 2 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
PART 1. FIRE AND MARINE INSURANCE [1880 - 10108.1]
( Part 1 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
CHAPTER 2. The Fire Insurance Contract [2030 - 2097]
( Chapter 2 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
ARTICLE 1. Change of Risk [2030 - 2033]
( Article 1 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
2030.
An insurer is entitled to rescind a contract of fire insurance upon an alteration in the use or condition of the subject matter insured from that to which it is limited by the policy, when such alteration is made without the consent of the insurer by means within the control of the insured, and increases the risk.
(Enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145.)

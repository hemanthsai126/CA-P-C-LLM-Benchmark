CHAPTER 4. General Mutual Insurers [4010 - 4097.20]
( Chapter 4 added by Stats. 1963, Ch. 1772. )
ARTICLE 1. General Provisions [4010 - 4019]
( Article 1 added by Stats. 1963, Ch. 1772. )
4015.
With respect to the management, records, and affairs of a domestic mutual insurer qualified to transact any or all of the classes of insurance designated in Section 700.01, and except as otherwise provided in this chapter, a member of such mutual insurer shall have the same character of rights and relationship as a stockholder has toward a domestic stock insurer subject to the provisions of this code.
(Amended by Stats. 1975, Ch. 824.)

DIVISION 5. INSURANCE ADJUSTERS [14000 - 16032]
( Division 5 added by Stats. 1980, Ch. 1190, Sec. 11. )
CHAPTER 3. Emergency Disaster Assessment [16000 - 16032]
( Chapter 3 added by Stats. 1998, Ch. 510, Sec. 1. )
ARTICLE 1. General Provisions [16000 - 16002]
( Article 1 added by Stats. 1998, Ch. 510, Sec. 1. )
16001.
This chapter shall apply to all insurers insuring real or personal property in the state, and any other classes of insurers that the commissioner determines should participate in emergency disaster preparation and assistance under this chapter.
(Added by Stats. 1998, Ch. 510, Sec. 1.   Effective September 15, 1998.)

CHAPTER 5. County Mutual Fire Insurers [5050 - 7060]
( Chapter 5 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
ARTICLE 5. Risks [6040 - 6050]
( Article 5 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
6050.
Where the amount of insurance in policies already written equals the amount limited by the provisions of this article, no additional insurance shall be written by such insurer on country property within a radius of one hundred feet of an existing risk. Such radius shall continue at not less than seventy-five feet during the life of the policy, unless insurance within the radius is covered by reinsurance.
(Enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145.)

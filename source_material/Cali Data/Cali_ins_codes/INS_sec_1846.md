CHAPTER 8. Life and Disability Insurance Analysts [1831 - 1849]
( Heading of Chapter 8 amended by Stats. 1983, Ch. 962, Sec. 3.5. )
1846.
A licensee or applicant for a license under this article shall notify the commissioner, in writing, of any changes in the address from which he intends to conduct his business.
(Added by Stats. 1959, Ch. 4.)

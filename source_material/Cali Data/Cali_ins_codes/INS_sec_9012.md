PART 1. FIRE AND MARINE INSURANCE [1880 - 10108.1]

CHAPTER 6. County Mutual Fire Reinsurers [7080 - 9060]
( Chapter 6 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
ARTICLE 7. Cancellation of Membership [9010 - 9012]
( Article 7 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
9012.
The reinsurer may cancel or terminate any policy by giving the member ten days’ written notice and returning to it any excess of premium paid during the term of the policy over the cost of its reinsurance as measured by the rules or methods of admitted fire insurers issuing nonassessable policies on a reserve basis.
(Enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145.)

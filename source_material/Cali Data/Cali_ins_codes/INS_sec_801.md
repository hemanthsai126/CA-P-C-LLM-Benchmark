801.
The amendments made in any sections contained in this article by the Statutes of the 1969 Regular Session of the Legislature shall not repeal, rescind or affect the interpretation of Article 1.5 (commencing with Section 685) of Chapter 1 of Part 2 of Division 1 of, or Section 1750.5 of this code.

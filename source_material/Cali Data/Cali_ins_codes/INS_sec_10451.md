ARTICLE 2. Registration of Life Policies [10450 - 10463]
( Article 2 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
10451.
When an insurer elects to register any of its policies, it shall register every policy thereafter issued by it until it discontinues registration.
(Enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145.)

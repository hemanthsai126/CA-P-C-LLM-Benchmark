ARTICLE 4. The Policy [6010 - 6021]
( Article 4 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
6015.
The insurer may add to the policy any matter relating to its financial condition, directors, officers, members and history, and the address of its home office and principal office in the State.
(Enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145.)

CHAPTER 10A. Firemen's, Policemen's,or Peace Officers’ Benefit and Relief Associations [11400 - 11407]
( Chapter 10A added by Stats. 1935, Ch. 161. )
11404.
Such association shall not pay, promise or agree to pay, either directly or indirectly, any consideration of any nature for the solicitation or procuring of members or applications for membership.

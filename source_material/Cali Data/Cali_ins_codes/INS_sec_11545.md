PART 2. LIFE AND DISABILITY INSURANCE [10110 - 11549]

CHAPTER 14. Conversion of an Incorporated Mutual Life or Life and Disability Insurer into an Incorporated Stock Life Insurer Issuing Policies on a Reserve Basis [11535 - 11549]
( Chapter 14 added by Stats. 1983, Ch. 988, Sec. 1. )
ARTICLE 1. Authority and Requirements to Convert [11535 - 11549]
( Article 1 added by Stats. 1983, Ch. 988, Sec. 1. )
11545.
The commissioner shall have the authority from time to time, to make, amend and rescind such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this chapter. The commissioner shall also have the authority to charge and collect from the insurer for the actual amount of expenses reasonably incurred by the state in discharge of the commissioner’s duties hereunder.
(Added by Stats. 1983, Ch. 988, Sec. 1.)

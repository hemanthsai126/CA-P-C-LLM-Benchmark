ARTICLE 2. Formation and Organization [10040- 10040.]
( Article 2 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
10040.
Any domestic corporation of insurance underwriters, organized to discover and prevent fires and save life and property from fire and doing business within any municipal corporation of this State, may at its own expense maintain an underwriter’s corps.
(Enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145.)

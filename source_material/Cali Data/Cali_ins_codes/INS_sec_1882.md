CHAPTER 1. The Marine Contract [1880 - 2010]

ARTICLE 1. Insurable Interests Peculiar to Marine Insurance [1880 - 1886]
( Article 1 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
1882.
Freightage, in marine insurance, signifies all the benefit derived by the owner, either from the chartering of the ship or its employment for the carriage of his own goods or those of others.
(Enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145.)

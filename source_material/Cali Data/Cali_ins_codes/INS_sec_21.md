Insurance Code - INS
GENERAL PROVISIONS
( General Provisions enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
21.
“Division,” and “department,” in reference to the government of this state, mean the Department of Insurance of this state.
(Amended by Stats. 1990, Ch. 1239, Sec. 4.)

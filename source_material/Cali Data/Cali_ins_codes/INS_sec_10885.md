CHAPTER 9. The Private Health Care Voluntary Purchasing Alliance Act [10800 - 10887]
( Chapter 9 added by Stats. 1996, Ch. 916, Sec. 1. )
ARTICLE 10. Exemptions [10885 - 10887]
( Article 10 added by Stats. 1996, Ch. 916, Sec. 1. )
10885.
Purchasing alliances shall be exempt from requirements of licensure as a health care service plan or solicitor under Chapter 2.2 (commencing with Section 1340) of Division 2 of the Health and Safety Code.
(Added by Stats. 1996, Ch. 916, Sec. 1.   Effective January 1, 1997.)

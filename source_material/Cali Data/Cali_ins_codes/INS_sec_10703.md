ARTICLE 2. Small Employer Carrier Requirements [10702 - 10718.7]
( Article 2 added by Stats. 1992, Ch. 1128, Sec. 10. )
10703.
The commissioner shall have the authority to determine whether a health benefit plan is covered by this chapter, and to determine whether an employer is a small employer within the meaning of Section 10700.
(Added by Stats. 1992, Ch. 1128, Sec. 10.   Effective January 1, 1993.   Operative July 1, 1993, by Sec. 15 of Ch. 1128.)

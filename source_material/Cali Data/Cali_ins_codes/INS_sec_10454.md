ARTICLE 2. Registration of Life Policies [10450 - 10463]
( Article 2 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
10454.
An insurer registering policies shall maintain a special deposit of securities with the commissioner for the benefit of such registered policies. Such securities shall be of the character specified in Articles 3, 4 and 6 of Chapter 2, Part 2, Division 1, or specified in sections 10459 and 10460.

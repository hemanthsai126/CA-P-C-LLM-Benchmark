CHAPTER 11. Change by Assessment Plan Insurer to Reserve Plan [11420 - 11490]
( Chapter 11 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
ARTICLE 2. Capital Adjustments [11440 - 11443]
( Article 2 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
11440.
Any insurer so reorganized shall have assets representing a paid-in capital represented by shares of stock apart from any assets described in Section 11441. At least two hundred thousand dollars ($200,000) of that capital shall be paid up before the insurer issues any policies on the reserve plan.
(Amended by Stats. 1982, Ch. 454, Sec. 115.)

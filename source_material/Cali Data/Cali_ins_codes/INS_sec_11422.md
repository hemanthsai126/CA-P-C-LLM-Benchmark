ARTICLE 1. Authorization and Effect [11420 - 11422]
( Article 1 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
11422.
The transformed insurer is a continuation of the original insurer. Its officers elected before or during the transformation serve through their respective terms as provided in the articles and by-laws under which they were elected. Their successors shall be elected and serve as the law and its articles and by-laws provide after the transformation. Such transformation shall not affect existing suits, rights or contracts.
(Enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145.)

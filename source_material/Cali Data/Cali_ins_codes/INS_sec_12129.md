12129.
The provisions of this chapter shall be enforced by the commissioner, and he may, after notice and public hearing, promulgate such reasonable rules and regulations as are necessary to administer this chapter.
(Added by renumbering Section 12124 by Stats. 1995, Ch. 91, Sec. 104.   Effective January 1, 1996.)

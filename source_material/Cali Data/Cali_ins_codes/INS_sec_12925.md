Insurance Code - INS
DIVISION 3. THE INSURANCE COMMISSIONER [12900 - 13855]
( Division 3 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
CHAPTER 2. Powers and Duties [12919 - 13555]
( Chapter 2 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
ARTICLE 1. Generally [12919 - 12938]
( Article 1 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
12925.
The commissioner shall keep and preserve in a permanent form a full record of the commissioner’s proceedings, including a concise statement of the condition of each insurer, surplus line broker, or motor club examined as to their condition and affairs.
(Amended by Stats. 2021, Ch. 50, Sec. 250.   (AB 378)   Effective January 1, 2022.)

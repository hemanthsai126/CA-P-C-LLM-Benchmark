CHAPTER 4. The Policy [380 - 460]
( Chapter 4 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
ARTICLE 1. Definition and Scope [380 - 396]
( Article 1 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
392.
Any exception from the risk generally covered by a policy of insurance which insures property for the period of time when such property is under the dominion and control of a party other than the insured for the purpose of storage or for the purpose of transferring such property to another location shall be printed in at least eight-point blackface type.
(Added by Stats. 1968, Ch. 135.)

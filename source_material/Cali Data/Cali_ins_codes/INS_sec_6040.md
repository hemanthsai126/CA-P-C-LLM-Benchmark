ARTICLE 5. Risks [6040 - 6050]

6040.
Such insurer may issue policies on dwellings, schoolhouses, churches, fraternal or lodge halls, community, creamery, or farm buildings and property contained in or on such premises or owned by the insured and stored in public or private warehouses.
(Amended by Stats. 1955, Ch. 1904.)

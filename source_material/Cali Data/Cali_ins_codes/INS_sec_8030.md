ARTICLE 3. Additional Memberships [8030 - 8031]
( Article 3 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
8030.
Any county mutual fire insurer in this State may become a member of such a reinsurer and entitled to all the rights and privileges appertaining thereto.
(Enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145.)

CHAPTER 5. County Mutual Fire Insurers [5050 - 7060]
( Chapter 5 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
ARTICLE 5. Risks [6040 - 6050]
( Article 5 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
6044.
All such insurers shall classify the subject matter insured by them at the time of issuing policies thereon. Such classification shall be under rates corresponding as nearly as practicable to the greater or less risk from loss attached to the several kinds of subject matter insured.
(Amended by Stats. 1939, Ch. 931.)

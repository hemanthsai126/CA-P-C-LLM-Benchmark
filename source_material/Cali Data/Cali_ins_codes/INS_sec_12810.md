Insurance Code - INS
DIVISION 2. CLASSES OF INSURANCE [1880 - 12880.8]
( Division 2 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
PART 8. SERVICE CONTRACTS [12800 - 12865]
( Part 8 added by Stats. 2003, Ch. 439, Sec. 5. )
12810.
(a) No person, other than a seller, shall sell or offer for sale a vehicle service contract to a purchaser.
(b) No obligor shall use a seller as a fronting company and no seller shall act as a fronting company.  For purposes of this section, a “fronting company” is a seller that authorizes a third-party obligor to use its name or business to evade or circumvent the provisions of subdivision (a).
(Added by Stats. 2003, Ch. 439, Sec. 5.   Effective January 1, 2004.   Operative July 1, 2004, by Sec. 7 of Ch. 439.)

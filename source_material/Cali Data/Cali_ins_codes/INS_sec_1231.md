ARTICLE 5.5. Life Insurance Policy Loans [1230 - 1239.5]
( Article 5.5 added by Stats. 1982, Ch. 1351, Sec. 3. )
1231.
For the purposes of this article the “published monthly average” means:
(a) Moody’s Corporate Bond Yield Average-Monthly Average Corporates as published by Moody’s Investors Service, Inc. or any successor thereto.
(b) In the event that Moody’s Corporate Bond Yield Average-Monthly Average Corporates is no longer published, a substantially similar average, established by the Legislature.
(Added by Stats. 1982, Ch. 1351, Sec. 3.)

DIVISION 3. THE INSURANCE COMMISSIONER [12900 - 13855]
( Division 3 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
CHAPTER 1. Appointment, Qualification, and Offices [12900 - 12907]
( Chapter 1 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
12907.
The following existing positions in the Department of Insurance shall be appointed by the Governor and are exempt from the state civil service system:
(a) Chief executive officer.
(b) Deputy commissioner for the office of the ombudsman.
(c) Career executive assignment IV, in the administration and licensing services division.
(Added by Stats. 2002, Ch. 1124, Sec. 38.   Effective September 30, 2002.)

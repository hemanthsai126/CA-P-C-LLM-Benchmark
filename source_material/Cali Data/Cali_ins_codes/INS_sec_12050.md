CHAPTER 1. Surety Insurers on Reserve Basis; Capital Requirements and Permitted Insurances [12050 - 12122]
( Chapter 1 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
ARTICLE 1. Capital and Scope of Business [12050 - 12055]
( Article 1 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
12050.
An incorporated insurer issuing surety policies on the reserve basis shall be governed by the paid-in capital and surplus requirements of Sections 700. 01 to 700.05, inclusive.
(Amended by Stats. 1953, Ch. 958.)

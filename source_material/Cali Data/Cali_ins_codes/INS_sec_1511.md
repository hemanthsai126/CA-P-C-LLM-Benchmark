1511.
For the purpose of organization, and upon issuance of permit by the commissioner, powers of attorney and applications for such policies may be solicited without compliance with the provisions of this chapter, but no attorney, agent or other person shall execute any such policies until all of the provisions of this chapter are complied with.
(Enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145.)

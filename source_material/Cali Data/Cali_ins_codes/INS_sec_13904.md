Insurance Code - INS
DIVISION 4. AFFORDABLE HOUSING ENTITIES RISK RETENTION POOL [13900 - 13907]
( Division 4 added by Stats. 2010, Ch. 384, Sec. 1. )
13904.
Any insurance pool, established pursuant to this division, shall not insure against any liability that may be insured against pursuant to Division 4 (commencing with Section 3200) of the Labor Code.
(Added by Stats. 2010, Ch. 384, Sec. 1.   (AB 2327)   Effective January 1, 2011.)

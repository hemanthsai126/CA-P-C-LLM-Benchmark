ARTICLE 10. Financial Statements of Insurers [900 - 925.4]
( Article 10 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
903.
The commissioner shall require statements and reports to be verified as follows: (a) If made by a domestic corporation, by the oaths of any two of the executive officers thereof. (b) If made by an individual or firm, by the oath of such individual or a member of the firm. (c) If made by a foreign insurer, by the oath of the principal executive officer thereof, or manager, residing within the United States.
(Enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145.)

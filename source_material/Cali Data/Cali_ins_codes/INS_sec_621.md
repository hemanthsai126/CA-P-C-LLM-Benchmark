DIVISION 1. GENERAL RULES GOVERNING INSURANCE [100 - 1879.8]
( Division 1 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
PART 1. THE CONTRACT [100 - 679.75]
( Part 1 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
CHAPTER 8. Reinsurance [620 - 623]
( Chapter 8 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
621.
A reinsurance is presumed to be a contract of indemnity against liability, and not merely against damage.
(Enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145.)

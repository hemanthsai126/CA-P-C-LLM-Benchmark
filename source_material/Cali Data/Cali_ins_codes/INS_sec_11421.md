CHAPTER 11. Change by Assessment Plan Insurer to Reserve Plan [11420 - 11490]

ARTICLE 1. Authorization and Effect [11420 - 11422]
( Article 1 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
11421.
After completing such transformation and procuring from the commissioner a certificate of authority to transact business in this State as an incorporated insurer issuing policies on a reserve basis, it may incur the obligations and enjoy the benefits of such insurer.

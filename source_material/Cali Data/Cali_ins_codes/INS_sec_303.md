ARTICLE 5. Insurable Interest: Effect of Transfer [300 - 305]
( Article 5 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
303.
A change of interest by will or succession, on the death of the insured, does not avoid insurance; and his interest in the insurance passes to the person taking his interest in the subject matter insured.
(Enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145.)

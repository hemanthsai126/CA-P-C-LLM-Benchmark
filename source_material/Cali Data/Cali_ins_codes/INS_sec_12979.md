DIVISION 3. THE INSURANCE COMMISSIONER [12900 - 13855]
( Division 3 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
CHAPTER 2. Powers and Duties [12919 - 13555]
( Chapter 2 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
ARTICLE 4. Fees [12970 - 12980]
( Article 4 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
12979.
Notwithstanding the provisions of Section 12978, the commissioner shall establish a schedule of filing fees to be paid by insurers to cover any administrative or operational costs arising from the provisions of Article 10 (commencing with Section 1861.01) of Chapter 9 of Part 2 of Division 1.
(Added November 8, 1988, by initiative Proposition 103, Sec. 5.)

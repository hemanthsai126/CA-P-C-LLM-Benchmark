DIVISION 2. CLASSES OF INSURANCE [1880 - 12880.8]
( Division 2 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
PART 2. LIFE AND DISABILITY INSURANCE [10110 - 11549]
( Part 2 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
CHAPTER 2. Group Life Policies [10200 - 10214]
( Chapter 2 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
10212.
Except as provided in this chapter, in every group policy issued by a domestic life insurer, the employer is the policyholder for all purposes within the meaning of this code. If entitled to vote at meetings of the insurer, he shall be entitled to one vote.
(Enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145.)

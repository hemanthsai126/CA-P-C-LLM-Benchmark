ARTICLE 1. Capital and Scope of Business [12050 - 12055]
( Article 1 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
12055.
An insurer admitted in the State to transact credit insurance may also acquire and dispose of debts so insured and collect any debts owed to such insurer or to any person so insured by it. In performing any such act the credit insurer shall be under the jurisdiction of the Insurance Commissioner.
(Added by Stats. 1963, Ch. 19.)

DIVISION 5. INSURANCE ADJUSTERS [14000 - 16032]
( Division 5 added by Stats. 1980, Ch. 1190, Sec. 11. )
CHAPTER 1. Insurance Adjuster Act [14000 - 14099]
( Chapter 1 added by Stats. 1980, Ch. 1190, Sec. 11. )
ARTICLE 4. Bonds [14050 - 14054]
( Article 4 added by Stats. 1980, Ch. 1190, Sec. 11. )
14051.
The bond required by this article shall be taken in the name of the people of this state and every person injured by the willful, malicious, or wrongful act of the principal may bring an action on the bond in his or her own name to recover damages suffered by reason of such willful, malicious, or wrongful act.
(Added by Stats. 1980, Ch. 1190, Sec. 11.   Operative July 1, 1981, by Sec. 13 of Ch. 1190.)

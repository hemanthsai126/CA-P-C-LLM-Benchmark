CHAPTER 8. Life and Disability Insurance Analysts [1831 - 1849]
( Heading of Chapter 8 amended by Stats. 1983, Ch. 962, Sec. 3.5. )
1845.
Any person who acts, offers to act or assumes to act as a life and disability insurance analyst in violation of Section 1844 is guilty of a public nuisance and may be enjoined from so doing pursuant to Section 12928.6.
(Amended by Stats. 1983, Ch. 962, Sec. 12.5.)

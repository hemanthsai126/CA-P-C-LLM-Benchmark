ARTICLE 5.5. Insurance in Connection With Sales and Loans [770 - 776]
( Article 5.5 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 224. )
776.
No person who sells real property shall require, as a condition precedent to the sale of such real property, that the person buying the real property negotiate any insurance or renewal thereof covering such property through a particular insurance agent, insurance broker, or insurance solicitor.
(Added by Stats. 1969, Ch. 302.)

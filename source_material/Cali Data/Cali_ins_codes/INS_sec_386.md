ARTICLE 1. Definition and Scope [380 - 396]
( Article 1 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
386.
All policies issued by incorporated insurers shall be subscribed by the president or vice president, or chairman, or chief executive officer, or, in case of the death, absence, or disability of those officers, by any two of the directors, and countersigned by the secretary of the corporation. All such policies are as binding and obligatory upon the corporation as if executed over the corporate seal.
(Amended by Stats. 1990, Ch. 539, Sec. 1.)

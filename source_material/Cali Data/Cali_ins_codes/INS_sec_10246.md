DIVISION 2. CLASSES OF INSURANCE [1880 - 12880.8]
( Division 2 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
PART 2. LIFE AND DISABILITY INSURANCE [10110 - 11549]
( Part 2 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
CHAPTER 3. Burial Contracts [10240 - 10252]
( Chapter 3 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
10246.
All funeral insurance contracts shall be subject in all respects to the provisions of Sections 10150 to 10167 inclusive, and of Sections 10478 to 10489.6 inclusive of this code.
(Added by Stats. 1947, Ch. 557.)

100.
Insurance in this state is divided into the following classes:
(1) Life.
(2) Fire.
(3) Marine.
(4) Title.
(5) Surety.
(6) Disability.
(7) Plate glass.
(8) Liability.
(9) Workers’ compensation.
(10) Common carrier liability.
(11) Boiler and machinery.
(12) Burglary.
(13) Credit.
(14) Sprinkler.
(15) Team and vehicle.
(16) Automobile.
(17) [Reserved]
(18) Aircraft.
(19) Mortgage guaranty.
(19.5) Insolvency.
(19.6) Legal insurance.
(20) Miscellaneous.

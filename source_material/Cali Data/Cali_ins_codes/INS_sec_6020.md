DIVISION 2. CLASSES OF INSURANCE [1880 - 12880.8]

CHAPTER 5. County Mutual Fire Insurers [5050 - 7060]
( Chapter 5 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
ARTICLE 4. The Policy [6010 - 6021]
( Article 4 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
6020.
Except as otherwise provided by this article, clauses may be attached, by separate riders in type larger than pica or in capital letters measuring not less than eight one-hundredths (8/100's) of an inch in height, to the policy, imposing specified duties and obligations upon the insured and limiting the liability of the insurer.
(Amended by Stats. 1949, Ch. 697.)

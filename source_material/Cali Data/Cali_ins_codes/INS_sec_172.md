CHAPTER 2. Parties, Events, and Interests [150 - 305]
( Chapter 2 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
ARTICLE 2. Parties to Contract: Mortgagor and Mortgagee [170 - 172]
( Article 2 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
172.
If an insurer assents to the transfer of insurance from a mortgagor to a mortgagee, and, at the time of the assent, imposes further obligations on the assignee, the acts of the mortgagor cannot affect the rights of the assignee.
(Enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145.)

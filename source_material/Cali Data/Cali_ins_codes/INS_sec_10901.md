PART 2. LIFE AND DISABILITY INSURANCE [10110 - 11549]

CHAPTER 9.5. Individual Access to Contracts for Health Care Services [10900 - 10902.5]
( Chapter 9.5 added by Stats. 2000, Ch. 810, Sec. 5. )
10901.
Every carrier offering health benefit plans to individuals shall comply with the provisions of this chapter and the rules adopted thereunder.
(Added by Stats. 2000, Ch. 810, Sec. 5.   Effective January 1, 2001.)

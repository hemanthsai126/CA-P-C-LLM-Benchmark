CHAPTER 7. Bail Licenses [1800 - 1823]

ARTICLE 1. Qualification and Licensing [1800 - 1823]
( Article 1 added by Stats. 1937, Ch. 654. )
1804.
An applicant for bail license shall file with the commissioner an application in such form and having such supporting documents as the commissioner prescribes, except that the application shall be verified in the manner provided for verification of complaints in civil cases. The commissioner shall investigate the licensees in such manner and in respect to such matters as he deems advisable.
(Added by Stats. 1937, Ch. 654.)

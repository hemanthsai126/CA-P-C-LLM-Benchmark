Insurance Code - INS
GENERAL PROVISIONS
( General Provisions enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
23.
The person who undertakes to indemnify another by insurance is the insurer, and the person indemnified is the insured.
(Enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145.)

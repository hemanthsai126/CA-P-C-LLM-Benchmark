DIVISION 3. THE INSURANCE COMMISSIONER [12900 - 13855]
( Division 3 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
CHAPTER 2. Powers and Duties [12919 - 13555]
( Chapter 2 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
ARTICLE 4. Fees [12970 - 12980]
( Article 4 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
12972.
The commissioner shall require the payment of one dollar ($1), in advance, as a fee for attaching the commissioner’s seal of office to any paper or document not specified in this code.
(Amended by Stats. 2021, Ch. 50, Sec. 257.   (AB 378)   Effective January 1, 2022.)

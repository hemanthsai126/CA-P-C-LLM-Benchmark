CHAPTER 5. County Mutual Fire Insurers [5050 - 7060]
( Chapter 5 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
ARTICLE 4. The Policy [6010 - 6021]
( Article 4 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
6021.
It is a misdemeanor for any insurer or its agent to countersign or issue a county mutual fire insurance policy covering in whole or part subject-matter in this State in violation of this article. Any policy so issued shall notwithstanding be binding upon the issuing insurer.
(Enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145.)

CHAPTER 8. Life and Disability Insurance Analysts [1831 - 1849]
( Heading of Chapter 8 amended by Stats. 1983, Ch. 962, Sec. 3.5. )
1839.
The commissioner shall conduct or arrange for a written examination, to be given at least twice a year upon questions prepared by the commissioner, as to the qualifications of the applicant to act as a life and disability insurance analyst.
(Amended by Stats. 1983, Ch. 962, Sec. 7.5.)

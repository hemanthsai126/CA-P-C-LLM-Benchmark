DIVISION 3. THE INSURANCE COMMISSIONER [12900 - 13855]
( Division 3 enacted by Stats. 1935, Ch. 145. )
CHAPTER 2. Powers and Duties [12919 - 13555]

ARTICLE 7. Suspension of Privilege of Appearing or Practicing Before the Commissioner [13500 - 13505]
( Article 7 added by Stats. 1986, Ch. 1328, Sec. 7. )
13505.
All hearings held under this article shall be closed to the public, unless the commissioner on the commissioner’s own motion, or at the request of a party, otherwise directs.
(Amended by Stats. 2021, Ch. 50, Sec. 260.   (AB 378)   Effective January 1, 2022.)

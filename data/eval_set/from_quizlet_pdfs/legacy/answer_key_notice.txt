About answers.txt (Quizlet Test PDFs)
--------------------------------------

The printed PDFs do **not** include which letter (A–D) was correct for each item.

We inferred letters by:

  1. Keeping the same **Quizlet sets** as in the PDF URLs (cached HTML dumps in
     `data/quizlet/quizlet_ca_flashcard_dump.md` and `data/quizlet/quizlet_ins_flashcard_dump.md`).
  2. Matching each `questions.txt` stem to a flashcard **term**, then matching the
     flashcard **definition** (the correct answer phrase) to the closest of the
     four **options** in that block.

Re-run or refresh answers:

  .venv/bin/python3 scripts/fill_answers_from_quizlet_flashcards.py \\
    --questions eval_set/from_quizlet_pdfs/questions.txt \\
    --out eval_set/from_quizlet_pdfs/answers.txt

Rows that stay `?` mean no confident flashcard stem match or no clear option match
(often due to OCR/spacing differences). Fix those manually with
`--answers-merge-from` on `scripts/quizlet_pdfs_to_eval_txt.py`, or edit
`answers_key_TEMPLATE.tsv` and merge.

INS QAA-style PDFs (numbered 1–4 options + duplicate answer line) still get keys
directly from the PDF when you include them in `--pdfs`.

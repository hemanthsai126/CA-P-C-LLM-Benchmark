California Department of Insurance (CADI) Licensing Exam

The California Department of Insurance (CADI) licensing exam is essential for professionals seeking to work in the insurance industry in California. Successfully passing this exam grants you the necessary credentials to legally practice as an insurance agent, broker, or adjuster in the state.

About the CADI Exam

Take your CADI Licensing exam with confidence. At PSI, we’re committed to helping you succeed. Our dedicated team is here to support you throughout your testing journey, ensuring an easy scheduling process and a dependable testing experience. Choose the convenience of online testing or the familiarity of a local test center, whichever works best for you.

The CADI exam evaluates your understanding of California’s insurance laws, ethics, and industry practices.

  • Exam format: Multiple-choice questions.
  • Duration: Varies by license type.
  • Eligibility: Candidates must meet CADI licensing requirements before scheduling the exam.

Exam locations and availability

The CADI licensing exam is available online and at approved test centers across California. Candidates applying for an insurance license must adhere to the guidelines set by the California Department of Insurance.

CADI Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule my CADI exam?

PSI provides two easy methods to schedule appointments. You may schedule your exam online, or via phone by calling our candidate support specialists.

What are the requirements for online testing?

Your success is our priority, so PSI has compiled essential tips and guides to help you succeed, whether online or in-person. To review all testing requirements, visit PSI’s Test Taker Resources.

What happens if I need to reschedule my exam?

Many test appointments can be cancelled and/or rescheduled through logging in to your account. Check the CADI FAQs or Candidate Information Bulletin for your program’s particular rescheduling rules.

For more details, visit our FAQ page.
